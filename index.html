Handly — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Handly ("we", "our", "the app") is a collaborative shopping list app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information — your full name and email address, provided when you create an account.
  • Household data — household names, member lists, shopping lists, and list items you create or are invited to.
  • Activity timestamps — when you last opened a household, used to show "active now" status to other household members.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To let you create, share, and collaborate on shopping lists with household members.
  • To display your name and initials to other members of your households.

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or tracking across other apps.

3. Third-Party Services

Handly uses the following Firebase (Google Cloud) services to operate:

  • Firebase Authentication — handles account creation and sign-in.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores household, shopping list, and user profile data.

Data is stored on Google Cloud servers. Google's privacy policy applies to their infrastructure: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Sharing

Your name and initials are visible to other members of households you belong to. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is kept as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app. This permanently removes:

  • Your user profile
  • Your membership in all households
  • Any households you own (including their shopping lists and items)

6. Security

All communication between the app and Firebase is encrypted in transit (TLS). Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication with industry-standard practices.

7. Children's Privacy

Handly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
